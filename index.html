About Us

PEC Group is a conglomerate of several social enterprises operating from PEC House in Ngong. They are driven by one common agenda- to provide services that transform individuals and groups into better people holistically- mentally, spiritually, physically, economically and socially.

PEC House was officially dedicated to the service of humanity in a colorful ceremony on 25 th March 2012. Since then, we have hosted thousands of local and international guests whose stay has always been transformative. We also provide services such as training and capacity building to local people in areas of personal development, peace and nature conservation so as to be of positive influence and socially responsible to the community we belong to.

PEC is an acronym for PEACE AND ENVIRONMENT CENTRE. The directors have a passion for sustainable development through peace and environmental conservation initiatives, which lead to the wellness of both people and the environment in which they live. By establishing PEC, they have a desire that every guest who walks through its doors will experience peace in the serenity that the environment provides and leave transformed and inspired to share the same vision for our world.

What We Offer

We are one-stop shop for all your wellness needs:

  • Comfortable accomodation
  • Sumptuous meals
  • Meeting and conferencing needs
  • Rest and recreation
  • Wellness through advice, massage, refloxology and training

Our Facilities
  • 34 standard size self-contained rooms fitted with double beds
  • 4 executive rooms fitted with double beds and kitchenette
  • 1 large conference room (100 pax)
  • 2 medium conference room (50 pax)
  • 1 Wellness Centre for activities like prayers & mindful breathing
  • 1 Massage Room
We can arrange the following local Activities:-
  • Adventure, camping or nature education at PEC Nature Camp
  • Hiking , picnic, retreat or nature trail to Ngong Hills
  • Tour to Nairobi National Park, Giraffe Centre, Elephant Orphanage
  • Tour to Maasai Manyattas or Women Groups
  • Tours and Excursions to other tourist sites like Ololua Nature Trail
  • Exchange programs for students & researchers
